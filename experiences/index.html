Hi! My name is Jamie Yu, and I'm a twenty year-old from Boston, Massachusetts. I am a rising junior in college, at Case Western Reserve University, located in Cleveland, Ohio. Ever since I was young, I knew that I loved to create. While I can't remember every detail of my childhood, one thing I do remember is watching my dad tinker around with old computer parts. My fascination for building and designing continued to grow as I realized that in high school, the classes that enjoyed the most involved problem-solving and creative thinking. For that reason, I decided to follow my father's footsteps and pursue and engineering degree in college, entering as a Biomedical Engineering student in 2014.
